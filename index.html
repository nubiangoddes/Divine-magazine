About Divine

A New Vision

Celebrating Black excellence, AI innovation, and the divine power in every soul.

Divine is not merely a magazine. It is a portal — where fashion is not worn but summoned, where style is not chosen but channeled from realms beyond the visible.

Created by visionary designer Natasha Dyson, founder of Nubian Goddess, Divine Magazine explores the intersection of ancestral wisdom and future innovation through luxury editorial storytelling, AI-designed fashion, and cultural empowerment.

Each issue is a cinematic experience designed for the divine in all of us — kings and queens, sovereigns and visionaries, warriors and alchemists. Because power is not bound by gender. It is claimed by those ready to rise.

— Natasha Dyson, Founder & Creative Director
